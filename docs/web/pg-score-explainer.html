Before Scoring: 3 Safety Gates

Every product must pass through three mandatory checks before any points are calculated. A failure stops scoring entirely.

1
Banned & Recalled Substance Check
Every ingredient is scanned against FDA, DEA, and international regulatory databases. Recalled or banned substances block scoring entirely.
2
Ingredient Mapping Verification
Every active ingredient must be recognized in our database. Unmatched ingredients mean the product is marked NOT_SCORED — we never guess.
3
Regression Guard
Prevents dangerous substances from slipping through as "mapping misses." If an unmatched ingredient matches a banned substance, scoring is halted.
